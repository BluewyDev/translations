Available Toggles

Logging (0) - Enables logging for checks performed when members join
Autoban (1) - Enables autoban for when members join
Anti-Spam (2) - Enables anti-spam
Risk Assessment* (3) - Enables risk assessment when members join

To view the list of toggles, run s?toggle

Enabling a Toggle:

To enable a toggle, use s?toggle <1-4> enable

Example: s?toggle 2 enable

Disabling a Toggle:

To enable a toggle, use s?toggle <1-4> disable

IExample: s?toggle 2 disable
